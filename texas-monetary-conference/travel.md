## Travel & Accommodation

---

<br/>

### Venue

The conference will be held at **Rice University** in Houston, Texas.

**Address:**<br/>
Department of Economics<br/>
Rice University<br/>
6100 Main Street<br/>
Houston, TX 77005

<br/>

### Getting to Houston

**By Air:**
Houston is served by two airports:
- **George Bush Intercontinental Airport (IAH)** — approximately 40 minutes from campus
- **William P. Hobby Airport (HOU)** — approximately 20 minutes from campus

**From the Airport:**
Taxi, rideshare (Uber/Lyft), and rental cars are available from both airports.

<br/>
